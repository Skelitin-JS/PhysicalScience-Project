Planet Booklet|Jupiter

Jupiter is the 5th planet from the sun.

Jupiter is 462 million miles from the sun.

Jupiter is similar to the sun in the way that it is mostly composed of Hydrogen and Helium.

If you look closely, you will see a red spot or "eye" on the planet.

The red spot is actually a huge, ongoing storm!
