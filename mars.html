Planet Booklet|Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun.

Mars is 133 million miles away from the sun.

Indications of water on Mars has been found, indicating at many years ago Mars may have had life.

Mars is composed of sillicon, iron, magnesium, and oxygen.

Elon Musk plans to land on Mars by the end of 2029.
